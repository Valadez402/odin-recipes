The Denver Omelet

Home

About the recipe:

The Denver omelet is a change of pace from a French omelet and known for its firmer texture and caramelized flavor.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp diced onion
  • 2 tbsp diced green bell pepper
  • salt and black pepper
  • 1/3 cup shredded mozzerella cheese
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper

Directions

  1. Beat eggs until combined, do not overbeat
  2. Melt butter in skillet over medium heat, add onion and bell pepper then season with salt and pepper. Cook and stir until onions soften, about 5 minutes.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low and pour in eggs. Mix briefly with spatula while shaking the pan to ensure ingredients are evenly distributed.
  4. Quickly run spatula along edges of omelet and sprinkle cheese and cayenne pepper over omelet.
  5. Cook, shaking the pan occasionally, until top is still wet but not runny, about 5 minutes. Use spatula to fold omelet in half and transfer to plate.
