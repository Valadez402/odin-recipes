About the recipe

Delicate crepes always create an impressive display, but making them is surprisingly easy. And because they play well with both sweet and savory fillngs, these super-thin French pancakes are versatile enough to serve for breakfast,lunch, dinner and dessert.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps:

  1. First beat the eggs like they owe you money then mix in with flour until soft. It will start off kind of thick but should soften up more the more you mix.
  2. Next you want to melt the butter in the microwave for about 30 seconds then quickly add and mix into your batter so it doesn't harden.
  3. Now you want mix the milk in starting with half a cup so it doesn't splash everywhere, then pour in the rest. The batter should be very watery.
  4. Optional but you can add 2 tbsp of vanilla for extra sweetness.
  5. Now it's time to start cooking! Use a pan on medium heat and use a 1/4 cup to pour batter into the pan.
    • You want the crepe to be thin to have the right texture
    • It's also good to get the heat and timing right when flipping the crepe
  6. You know it's ready to flip when you see the edges start to turn brown.
